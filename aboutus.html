Sariaya Multi-Purpose Cooperative

Sariaya Community Cooperative Credit Union, Inc. or Kilusang Bayan Pananalapi ng Sariaya (PD No. 175) was established last October 7,1963 by then founding chairman Dr. Urbano Reynoso and with other 15 incorporators and 97 members. As years goes by, the cooperative change its name to Sariaya Community Credit Cooperative, Inc. (RA 6938 & RA 939) and by the year 2011 and with RA 9520, it became what we know today as Sariaya Multi-Purpose Cooperative.

A Cooperative that is responsive to the needs of its members in the different aspects and sustain the economic growth of the community.

Vision

Upliftment of the quality of life of every member and also those who patronize the cooperative services.
